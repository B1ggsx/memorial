A Life of Service

For more than 50 years, Loretta Milner lived a life devoted to serving others. She was a woman of Christ—one of God’s most genuine and faithful souls—whose life reflected compassion, grace, humility, and unconditional love.

As a dedicated nurse, and throughout her 15 years as a hospice nurse, Loretta stood beside countless individuals and families during some of life’s hardest moments. She brought comfort where there was pain, peace where there was fear, and light where there was darkness. Her calling to care for others was not simply a profession—it was her ministry.
