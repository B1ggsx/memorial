A Lifetime of Service

Loretta Milner lived a life that was a testament to God’s grace. For over 50 years, she was a woman of Christ whose life reflected humility and unconditional love. As a dedicated nurse at St. Mary’s Hospital and through her 15 years in hospice care, Loretta brought peace to the suffering and light to the darkness. Her work was not just a profession—it was her ministry to the community.

Her Faith & Church

A dedicated member of Hills Chapel Baptist Church, Loretta poured her spirit into her faith and her family. She was the anchor for her kin, always placing the needs of others before her own. Whether at the bedside of a patient or in the pews of her church, she gave endlessly and loved deeply.
